Locums Nest

The NHS is spending £350 million annually (the equivalent of employing 11,000 extra full-time junior doctors) on agency fees alone in exchange for an inefficient process, seriously lacking integrity.

Medical locum spend has recently been in the spotlight with the media, hospital executives, the government and NHS bodies all weighing in. The NHS spends around £3billion a year on temporary medical and nursing staff and there are reports of locum doctors earning salaries above that of substantive staff - many think it’s all very excessive.
