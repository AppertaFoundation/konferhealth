Medelinked Personal Health Record

  • Patient Health Record, Treatment & Care Plans, Communicating, Monitoring & Measurement
  • Henley-On-Thames, UK

Medelinked is a secure mobile and online health platform that empowers you to take control of your health, from building your health profile to discovering, connecting to and sharing your health and wellness information with your trusted healthcare providers.


Medelinked allows you to research, locate, engage, introduce and automatically connect you, your family members and carers with healthcare providers, building your personal healthcare network from professionals listed in NHS Choices and other healthcare provider databases. It even helps you check symptoms before seeking consultations.
