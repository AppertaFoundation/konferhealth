Who are Locums Nest?

The NHS is spending £350million annually (the equivalent of employing 11,000 extra full-time junior doctors) on agency fees alone in exchange for an inefficient process, seriously lacking integrity.


Problem trying to solve

Being frontline NHS professionals ourselves, we appreciate the challenges posed by chronic understaffing and have made it our personal goal to help improve patient safety. Help us drive change and spread the word


Aims / Objectives

Help the NHS save money on agency fees when it comes to locum spend.
